# PXX cross-bike concept notes

PXX is the real name direction: **Pascal Cross Cross**.

`frankonpiler` is a nick / geuzennaam: a deliberately reclaimed nickname rather
than the formal product name. In English notes, "reclaimed nickname" is probably
clearer than trying to translate it as "GeusName".

Visual pun to explore:

- Pascal on a cross bike.
- At a crossing.
- "Cross cross" should read as both cross-compilation and a literal crossing.
- Could work as a logo mark, web hero image, sticker, or release-page artwork.

Avoid relying on generated text inside the image. If the final design needs
`PXX`, draw that as a separate vector/text layer later.

Initial image directions:

1. Logo/poster: stylized rider on a cyclocross bike at a road crossing, clean
   bold shapes, usable as a project identity image.
2. Web hero: wider scene with a rider paused at a crossing, compiler/toolchain
   symbolism in road signs and route lines, but no legible text.
3. Sticker/mascot: playful compact mark, rider + bike + crossing sign, simple
   enough to redraw as vector.
